About Swami Holiday Home

Your Gateway to Paradise at Sasawane Beach, Alibag

Our Story

Nestled along the pristine shores of Sasawane Beach in Alibag, Swami Holiday Home began with a simple vision – to create a sanctuary where guests could experience the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and authentic coastal hospitality.

Founded in 2015, our family-owned resort has grown from a small beach house to a beloved getaway destination. What started as a weekend retreat for friends and family soon became a dream to share the beauty of Alibag with travelers from around the world.

Today, we welcome guests with the same warmth and personal touch that has defined us from the beginning – treating every visitor like family.

Our Philosophy

At Swami Holiday Home, we believe that the best vacations are those where you can truly relax and connect with nature. Our property is designed to offer comfort without pretense – where the sound of waves replaces city noise and the stars shine brighter.

We're passionate about sharing the authentic flavors of the Konkan coast. Our in-house restaurant serves traditional Kokani and Aagri cuisine prepared with love and locally-sourced ingredients – because we believe food is an essential part of the travel experience.

Being pet-friendly is our way of ensuring that no family member gets left behind. We know that your furry friends are part of your family, and they're always welcome here.

The Location

Perched just 150 meters from Sasawane Beach, our resort offers the perfect base to explore the beauty of Alibag. Wake up to the sound of waves, enjoy morning walks on the beach, and witness spectacular sunsets every evening.

Our location puts you within easy reach of Alibag's most beloved attractions – from the historic Kolaba Fort to the serene Kihim Beach and the artistic Karmarkar Museum. Whether you're seeking adventure or relaxation, everything is just a short drive away.
